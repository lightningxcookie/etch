Etch Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Etch for iPhone, iPad and any other devices and platforms.

Information collected

Etch does not collect any personal user information. The only data it stores is user-generated drawings through the iMessage extension. This is used for the core multiplayer functionality of sharing drawings with the other players. These drawings are not linked to specific users.

User Consent

By using our product, you agree to our privacy policy.
